# Backlog - Website Improvements

## Core Features
- **Advanced search**: Filter by platform, release year range, rating range, multiplayer support
- **Game comparison tool**: Compare 2-3 games side-by-side with specs, ratings, user reviews
- **Wishlist/Backlog separation**: Distinguish between "want to play soon" vs "someday maybe"
- **Smart recommendations**: "Based on games you played/rated highly, try these"
- **Game alerts**: Notify when wishlist games go on sale or get updates

## Social & Community
- **Follow system**: See friends' activity, what they're playing, their reviews
- **Game clubs/groups**: Create/join groups for specific games, genres, or playstyles
- **Review comments**: Allow discussions on reviews
- **Upvote/downvote reviews**: Surface best community content
- **Share to social**: One-click share of reviews/ratings

## Content & Discovery
- **Game detail pages**: Full info with screenshots, trailers, similar games, metacritic
- **News/articles section**: Gaming news, reviews, opinion pieces
- **Developer/publisher profiles**: Browse by studio, see all their games
- **Achievement tracking**: Show off game achievements and completion rates

## UX/UI Improvements
- **Dark/light theme toggle**: User preference
- **Better mobile experience**: Bottom nav, touch-friendly cards
- **Infinite scroll**: For game discovery instead of pagination
- **Game cards with more info**: Show tags, metascore, platforms on hover
- **Quick actions**: Add to library from discovery with one click

## Technical Enhancements
- **PWA functionality**: Offline support, install as app
- **Image optimization**: Lazy loading, WebP formats, CDN
- **Better error states**: Skeleton loaders, error boundaries
- **Analytics dashboard**: Track personal gaming habits, time spent

## Data Integration
- **Import from platforms**: Steam, Xbox, PlayStation, Nintendo Switch
- **Export user data**: Backup your library, reviews, ratings
- **Game deals API**: Show current prices from Steam, Epic, etc.

## Monetization (if desired)
- **Pro tier**: Advanced analytics, custom themes, no ads
- **Game deals**: Affiliate links to purchase games
- **Curated lists**: Featured collections by editors or influencers
